You can select which accounts emails are sent to (default is all listed accounts). Any accounts that are currently Off (not operational) are listed in lighter font, with "(off)" appended to the Live/Test indicator.

For convenience publisher/institution email addresses are displayed on the form below in the protected To (bcc) and Cc (bcc) fields. However, they are actually put in the email's BCC field when sending so that recipients will NOT see them.

Any email addresses entered in the Cc field will populate the email's actual CC address field.

Emails are sent from account: XXXX.YYYY@YYYY.ac.uk, and this same email address is the only one that will appear in the email's To address field.

Historic emails marked as deleted are purged from database if they are at least 12 months old.
