Further information

New publishers will need to successfully complete a period of validation testing of their submissions to Publications Router (by SFTP or API) before their account will be made live.

An automated process will check each submission and a report of errors and issues encountered will be produced and emailed to the publisher's nominated email addresses.

Files submitted by SFTP will be processed sooner if the SFTP client disconnects after a batch of files has been deposited. While an SFTP connection remains active, Router waits at least 30 minutes to process a file to avoid handling it while it is still being deposited.

During automated testing, notifications will only be validated and NOT processed as normal UNLESS the Route notifications checkbox is ticked.

When Route notifications is active then submissions which are free of severe errors will be saved and routed to Jisc-auto test repositories (those with Organisation name starting "Jisc-auto"), but they will NOT be recorded in the Publisher's statistics or submission history.
